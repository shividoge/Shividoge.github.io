How to Apply

We're thrilled that you're interested in joining our team at RoboHorizons! To kickstart your application process, head over to our website and find our user-friendly online application form. Here's a simple guide to help you navigate through the steps:

  1. Complete the Application Form: Start by filling in your first name, email address, and the specific position you're applying for. These basic details help us tailor the application process to your unique profile.
  2. Share Your Professional Profile: Include your LinkedIn URL to provide us with additional insights into your professional background. This step allows us to understand your skills and experiences more comprehensively.
  3. Craft Your Cover Letter: Take a moment to share your story. Let us know about your motivations, achievements, and why you believe you're the perfect fit for the role.
  4. Submit Your CV: Upload your CV in either PDF or DOCX format. This document is essential for us to assess your qualifications thoroughly.

Important Reminders: Ensure that each field in the form is accurately filled out to facilitate a smooth processing of your application. Please note that we only accept CVs in PDF or DOCX format, so make sure your CV is up-to-date and effectively showcases your skills.

What Happens Next: Once you've submitted your application, our dedicated team will carefully review the information provided. Expect to receive an email from us within 2-3 business days, outlining the next steps in the application process. We appreciate your patience as we strive to make this process transparent and efficient.

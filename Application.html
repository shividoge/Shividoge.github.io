How to Apply

We're thrilled that you're interested in joining our team at RoboHorizons! To kickstart your application process, head over to our website and find our user-friendly online application form. Here's a simple guide to help you navigate through the steps:

  • Complete the Application Form: Start by filling in your first name, email address, and the specific position you're applying for. These basic details help us tailor the application process to your unique profile.

  • Share Your Professional Profile: Include your LinkedIn URL to provide us with additional insights into your professional background. This step allows us to understand your skills and experiences more comprehensively.

  • Craft Your Cover Letter: Take a moment to share your story. Let us know about your motivations, achievements, and why you believe you're the perfect fit for the role.

  • Submit Your CV: Upload your CV in either PDF or DOCX format. This document is essential for us to assess your qualifications thoroughly.
